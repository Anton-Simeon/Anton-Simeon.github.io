WHAT WE DO

Why choose us?

Eclipse recognises today’s exposures present unique risk profiles, face different hazards and risk outcomes are not binary. That is why we focus on managing individual risk, rather than being governed by rigid business rules.

Our diverse but disciplined underwriting strategy is supported by technology and data analytics, promoting informed decision making and optimised insurance solutions.

At our core, our goal is to challenge the traditional legacy processes, practices, and technologies to create a unique customer-first experience. Our data-driven tools and philosophy augment – rather than replace – human judgment.

The Eclipse Advantage

Our business model begins with our customers at the central pillar, anchored by trust, transparency and partnership. We believe that by being transparent and equipping our clients with information, helps raise risk awareness and form closer relationships.

We have a diverse but disciplined underwriting strategy, supported by a technology enabled platform with strong data analytics capability, which promotes informed decision making and optimised solutions for your clients.

Our people have a wealth of industry experience spanning over 100+ years across Australian and global markets across different facets of insurance, who are not only technical and product experts but empowered decision makers.

We pride ourselves on being independent and a viable long-term alternative to traditional insurers. Eclipse is well capitalised with strong security, so you can be confident that Eclipse will be there for you when you need us most.

Lloyd’s of London

Lloyd’s is the world’s specialist insurance and reinsurance market.

With expertise earned over centuries, Lloyd’s is the foundation of the insurance industry and the future of it. Led by expert underwriters and brokers who cover more than 200 territories, the Lloyd’s market develops the essential, complex and critical insurance needed to underwrite human progress.

Backed by diverse global capital and excellent financial ratings, Lloyd’s works with a global network to grow the insured world – building resilience for businesses and local communities and strengthening economic growth around the world.
